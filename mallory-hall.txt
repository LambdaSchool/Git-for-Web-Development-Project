    1. What is Semantic HTML? 
        It is HTML that marks up the page and defines certain areas of the page and give meaning to things.

        HTML is used to structure a webpage and the content on that page.

        special words to control the behavior of elements, they are put in opening tags.
    4. What is the h1 tag used for? How many times should I use it on a page?
        h1 tags are used for the top/main header of a page, they should only be used once per page.
